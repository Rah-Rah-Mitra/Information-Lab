# Computer Vision Bibliography

*A comprehensive list of academic references covering algorithms and applications in computer vision, including image processing, 3D reconstruction, and object recognition.*

This bibliography provides a comprehensive collection of references for [[Computer Vision]], covering a wide array of algorithms and applications.

## Core Domains

The references span several key areas of research, including [[Image Processing]] for the manipulation of visual data, and [[3D Reconstruction]] for the recovery of three-dimensional structures from images.

## Key Algorithms and Techniques

Many of the cited works focus on [[Object Recognition]] and [[Image Segmentation]], which are fundamental for understanding scene content. Techniques such as [[Stereo Matching]] and [[Structure from Motion]] are frequently used to derive depth and spatial information.

Mathematical frameworks like [[Principal Component Analysis]] and [[Markov Random Fields]] are used for dimensionality reduction and probabilistic modeling, respectively. Other specialized methods include [[Bundle Adjustment]] for refining camera parameters, the [[Hough Transform]] for detecting geometric primitives, and [[Eigenfaces]] for facial recognition.
