# Digital Image Processing Bibliography

*A comprehensive list of foundational texts and research papers in digital image processing, computer vision, and signal analysis.*

This bibliography provides a curated collection of graduate-level references covering the breadth of [[Digital Image Processing]], [[Computer Vision]], and signal analysis.

## Core Domains

### Signal and Frequency Analysis
Foundational work in [[Fourier Analysis]] and [[Wavelet Transforms]] is central to image representation and compression, with key contributions from authors like Bracewell and Daubechies.

### Pattern and Shape Recognition
Research in [[Pattern Recognition]] and [[Computer Vision]] focuses on the extraction of geometric structures and the classification of shapes, often utilizing [[Linear Algebra]] for feature space analysis.

### Advanced Computational Methods
Modern approaches to image analysis are heavily influenced by [[Deep Learning]], particularly convolutional neural networks for classification and segmentation.

### Specialized Processing Techniques
The bibliography highlights specialized areas such as [[Image Restoration]] for deblurring and denoising, [[Morphological Image Analysis]] for shape-based processing, and [[Computed Tomography]] for 3D reconstruction from projections.
