# Computer Vision Bibliography and Index

*A comprehensive list of references and an alphabetical index of key concepts from a computer vision textbook.*

This document serves as the bibliography and index for a comprehensive textbook on [[Computer Vision]].

## Bibliography

The bibliography contains a wide array of seminal and contemporary research papers in the field of [[Computer Vision]], covering topics such as [[Principal Component Analysis]], [[Markov Random Fields]], and [[Graph Cuts]]. These references provide the theoretical foundation for many of the algorithms discussed throughout the text.

## Index of Concepts

The index provides a quick reference to key concepts, including:

### Image Analysis and Processing
- [[Image Segmentation]]: Techniques for partitioning an image into multiple segments, often utilizing [[Graph Cuts]] or [[Markov Random Fields]].
- [[Stereo Matching]]: The process of estimating depth from multiple images.
- [[Optical Flow]]: The pattern of apparent motion of objects between consecutive frames.

### Geometry and Projection
- [[Homography]]: A transformation that maps points from one plane to another.

### Rendering and Lighting
- [[Radiosity]] and [[Ray Tracing]]: Methods for simulating the transport of light in a scene to create realistic images.
