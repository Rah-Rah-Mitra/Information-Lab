# Computer Vision Bibliography

*A comprehensive list of academic references covering algorithms and applications in computer vision, image processing, and 3D reconstruction.*

This bibliography provides a curated list of foundational and contemporary research papers and textbooks in the field of [[Computer Vision]].

## Scope of References

The listed works cover a wide array of topics essential for graduate-level study in vision and graphics, including:

### Image Analysis and Processing
Research on [[Image Processing]] techniques such as [[Bilateral Filtering]], denoising, and radiometric calibration is extensively cited.

### Geometric Vision and 3D
Significant focus is placed on [[3D Reconstruction]], [[Stereo Vision]], and the recovery of shape from multiple views or silhouettes.

### Recognition and Segmentation
Numerous entries detail methods for [[Object Recognition]] and [[Image Segmentation]], often utilizing probabilistic models like [[Markov Random Fields]] or spectral clustering.

### Motion and Scale
Works on [[Optical Flow]] and the application of [[Scale-Space Theory]] for feature detection and edge analysis are central to the provided sources.
